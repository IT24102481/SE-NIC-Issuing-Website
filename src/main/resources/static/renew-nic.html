NIC RENEWAL SERVICE

Renew or Replace Your NIC

Use this service if your NIC is damaged, worn out, or if you need to upgrade to the new smart NIC format. Complete the form below to request a replacement.

When to Renew Your NIC

  • Your NIC is physically damaged or worn out
  • You need to update to the new smart NIC format
  • Your personal details have changed (use Update Details service instead)
  • Your NIC has expired (for certain older formats)

Note: If your NIC was lost or stolen, please use the Lost NIC service instead.
